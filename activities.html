Activities

FOOD SAVE ACT

With the help of NO FOOD WASTE and KAI KODU MANAMAEour GREEN CLUB MEMBERS initiated FOOD CONSERVATION ACT in our college to serve the empty stomach.The total food packs served till date was 3,649.


PLANT CULTIVATION

To make our environment greenery, we conducted lot of activities including planting trees. Some of the activities done by our green club

  • 450 Plants in ONE MINUTE
  • Saplings on GCT Alumni back side
  • Mass plantation SUPPORT DRIVE
  • Dustbin Plan

    • GREEN CLUB joins in hand with SIRUTHULI and ZF WIND POWER for the AFFORESTATION DRIVE in GCT. Around 3000 saplings of different varieties were provided by ZF Wind Power. For this project the wasteland of 1 acre was cleaned at GCT. The saplings were planted by Miyawaki method.


    CONSTRUCTION

    The 15 members of GREEN CLUB work hard for 14 hours without rest to construct a footpath on the way to canteen.This was the first construction done by Green Club.We collected the waste materials like beams from Civil department constructions and paver blocks from the open Auditorium.
